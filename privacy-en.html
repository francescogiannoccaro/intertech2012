Privacy Policy

Intertech values the relationship we have with our customers and website visitors and is committed to responsible and transparent information handling practices.

This Privacy Policy describes how Intertech collects, uses, stores, and shares information through its website and during offline interactions (for example, when purchasing products, registering at events, or contacting us directly).

You do not need to provide personal information to browse our website.

1. Information We Collect

This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected by Intertech or by carefully selected third parties acting on our behalf.

Personal information means any information that can reasonably be used to identify an individual.

We may collect personal information when you:

  • Purchase products or services
  • Register products online
  • Request customer support
  • Download software
  • Register for webinars or events
  • Request information (e.g., newsletters or white papers)
  • Participate in surveys, promotions, or contests
  • Apply for a job
  • Submit questions or comments

We may also collect information at trade shows, conferences, or other events (with your consent).

Personal information may include:

  • First and last name
  • Job title and company name
  • Postal address
  • Email address
  • Telephone number
  • Account credentials (e.g., username and password)
  • Payment details (when applicable)
  • Employment and education details (for job applicants)

For quality assurance and training purposes, communications with us may be monitored or recorded.

We may also collect technical information such as IP address, browser type, access times, referring website, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:

  • Provide products and services you request
  • Process transactions
  • Improve our website and services
  • Provide customer support
  • Conduct research and analysis
  • Communicate service-related updates (e.g., billing reminders, security notices)
  • Inform you about relevant products or services (where permitted by law)

Information may be stored and processed in countries where Intertech or its service providers operate. By using our website or services, you consent to such processing.

3. Sharing of Information

Intertech does not sell, rent, or lease personal information to third parties.

We may share information with:

  • Service providers acting on our behalf (e.g., hosting providers, payment processors, customer support services)
  • Business partners involved in fulfilling your request
  • Affiliates or subsidiaries of Intertech

All third-party service providers are required to handle information in accordance with confidentiality and data protection standards.

We may disclose personal information if required by law or when necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect and defend Intertech’s rights or property
  • Protect the safety of users or the public

4. Your Choices

  • Unsubscribe from newsletters at any time
  • Update or correct your information through registered account areas
  • Contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information (where legally applicable)

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational safeguards to protect personal information against unauthorised access, misuse, or disclosure.

Sensitive data transmitted through our website (such as payment information) is protected using secure encryption technologies.

If you create an account, you are responsible for keeping your password confidential. If you share a computer, always log out before leaving the site.

6. Cookies and Usage Data

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Enable website functionality
  • Remember preferences
  • Authenticate users
  • Analyse website usage in aggregate

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. You may configure your browser to refuse cookies; however, some website features may not function properly.

We may collect usage data such as IP address, date and time of access, referring pages, and browsing behaviour. This information is used for statistical and analytical purposes and is not used to identify you personally.

7. Links to Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. Intertech is not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Policy Updates

Intertech reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page. Significant changes will be highlighted on the homepage for a reasonable period.
