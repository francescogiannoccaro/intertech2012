Intertech is a company that operates from 2003 providing consultancy services in the field of Open Source and Enterprise Linux.
 
Intertech is the first company in Italy to have obtained the most important certifications in the open-source world, including:
 
  • RHCE (Red Hat Certified Engineer) - demonstrates the ability to configure network services and security on servers running Linux and has been honored by CertCities.com as the world's best IT certification
  • RHCA (Red Hat Enterprise Architect) - attests to the skills needed to plan, design and manage open source infrastructure in large and complex organizations, consisting of multiple Linux systems.
Working on frontier technologies, so far Intertech services have turned to large customers, especially banks, but also universities and research institutes (including for example the National Institute of Nuclear Physics).
This scenario puts an experience that has marked the history of our company. In 2004 Assofin (Association of Consumer Credit and Real Estate), asked Intertech the development of an e-learning platform to provide training services to their associates.
Intertech, starting from an open source code has developed in recent years "Methody" the first E-learning service provider in Europe, which offers a total outsourcing and pay per use solution.
The great flexibility of this service is linked to both the application solution developed by Intertech, and Cloud Computing.
